# ğŸ¯ **5. Types of Sampling (simple explanation)**

We will keep it very basic.

---

## **A. Random Sampling (everyone has equal chance)**

This is like picking chits from a bowl.

Example:
You randomly select 200 students from a school of 2000.

This is the most commonly used technique.

---

## **B. Stratified Sampling (pick from each group)**

If data has groups (gender, age, city), we take samples from each group.

Example:
If a population has:

* 60% male
* 40% female

We pick a sample that also has 60% male and 40% female.

This ensures fairness.

---

## **C. Systematic Sampling (pick every 10th item)**

For example:

* Pick every 5th person in a queue
* Pick every 10th record in a file

It is simple and fast.
