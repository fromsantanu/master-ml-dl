### **Signs of Underfitting**

* Not enough training
* Important features missing
* Wrong algorithm

## **ğŸŸ¦ 2. What is Overfitting?**

Overfitting happens when the model **learns too many details**, including noise, mistakes, and random patterns.

It is like:

* A student who memorizes the entire textbook word-for-word,
  but cannot explain anything in his own words.

He performs well on questions he memorized,
but fails on new questions.

### **Signs of Overfitting**

* Very high accuracy on training data
* Poor accuracy on test data
* Model is too complex

### **Causes**

* Too many features
* Too complex model
* Not enough data
* No regularization
* Model trained for too long
