## ğŸ” **5. How does t-SNE work? (Simple idea only)**

t-SNE works in two simple steps:

### **Step 1: Find neighbours**

For every point, t-SNE checks **who its closest friends are**.

Example:
Among 100 students, you find the handful of students most similar to each one.

### **Step 2: Place them in 2D so neighbours stay neighbours**

t-SNE then tries to arrange all points on a 2D sheet such that:

* Close friends are placed close
* Unrelated students are kept separate

This produces beautiful clusters.
